Integration of Rural GBV&F Prevention and Intervention.

In partnership with Akanani Rural Development Association (ARDA), we are conducting Community Dialogues and basic Domestic Violence training workshops to Traditional Authority Councils, as means of Raising Awareness and establishing a curbed Victim Secondary-victimization administration and referral system within villages.
